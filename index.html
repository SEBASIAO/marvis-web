The cost of developing a mobile app depends on complexity, features, and platforms (iOS, Android, or both). At Marvis Labs we analyze your project and provide a personalized, no-obligation quote.

The price of a professional website varies by type: a landing page, a corporate site, or an e-commerce platform each have different scopes. Factors include design complexity, number of pages, integrations (payments, CRM, analytics), and whether you need a CMS. Contact us for a free consultation tailored to your needs.

Creating a mobile app involves several stages: defining requirements, UI/UX design, development (frontend and backend), testing, and publishing to the App Store and Google Play. You don't need to be technical — at Marvis Labs we handle the entire process. You bring the idea, we build the app.

Timelines depend on the project scope. A professional website can be ready in 2-4 weeks. A mobile app MVP typically takes 2-3 months. More complex platforms with multiple integrations can take 4-6 months or more. We work with agile methodologies to deliver incremental value from the first weeks.

Custom software is built specifically for your business needs, unlike off-the-shelf solutions. It adapts to your unique processes, scales with your growth, and gives you a competitive advantage.
