FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Software Development

Everything you need to know about building apps, websites, and custom software.

The cost of developing a mobile app depends on complexity, features, and platforms (iOS, Android, or both). A basic app can start from a few thousand dollars, while complex platforms with backends, APIs, and integrations require a higher investment. At Marvis Labs we analyze your project and provide a personalized, no-obligation quote.

The price of a professional website varies by type: a landing page, a corporate site, or an e-commerce platform each have different scopes. Factors include design complexity, number of pages, integrations (payments, CRM, analytics), and whether you need a CMS. Contact us for a free consultation tailored to your needs.

Creating a mobile app involves several stages: defining requirements, UI/UX design, development (frontend and backend), testing, and publishing to the App Store and Google Play. You don't need to be technical — at Marvis Labs we handle the entire process. You bring the idea, we build the app.

Timelines depend on the project scope. A professional website can be ready in 2-4 weeks. A mobile app MVP typically takes 2-3 months. More complex platforms with multiple integrations can take 4-6 months or more. We work with agile methodologies to deliver incremental value from the first weeks.

Custom software is built specifically for your business needs, unlike off-the-shelf solutions. It adapts to your unique processes, scales with your growth, and gives you a competitive advantage. It's ideal when existing tools don't cover your requirements or when you need seamless integrations between systems.

Yes. We build native and cross-platform mobile applications for both iOS (iPhone, iPad) and Android. Using modern frameworks, we can develop for both platforms simultaneously, reducing costs and time to market while maintaining excellent performance and user experience.
